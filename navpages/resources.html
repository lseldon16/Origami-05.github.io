Resources

Origami resources I found very helpful.

Origami Instructions
This website has a lot of the basic origami models. This site is great for beginners as it also includes images for each step of the folding process.
Origami Resource Center
Origami Resource Center is just that. It has many models catalouged within the site which are categorized in several different way to aid in finding what you want to fold.
Jo Nakashima's Youtube Channel
Jo Nakashima's has many tutorials for folding models. A specific feature I like about his videos is that he includes the instructions for each step in the corner of the video which helps if you are having trouble understanding what he's doing with the paper.
